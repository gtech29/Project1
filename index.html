AI Literacy Through Interview Chatbots

This project explores how structured AI interview chatbots can support AI literacy, ethical reflection, and critical thinking in teacher preparation courses.

Project Details

The project involves the design and implementation of AI interview chatbots that simulate real-world scenarios. These chatbots are integrated into teacher preparation courses to provide students with opportunities to engage in ethical reflection and critical thinking about AI technologies.

Project Outcomes

Preliminary findings suggest that students who interacted with the AI interview chatbots demonstrated increased AI literacy and a deeper understanding of ethical considerations in AI. The project also highlighted the importance of incorporating AI ethics into teacher education programs.

Conclusion

Overall, the project underscores the potential of AI interview chatbots as a tool for enhancing AI literacy and fostering ethical reflection in teacher preparation courses. Future research will explore the long-term impact of these chatbots on students' understanding of AI and their ability to critically engage with AI technologies.
